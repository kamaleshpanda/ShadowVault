# 🌿 ShadowVault

> **A personal second brain for software engineering, data science research, academic growth, and interview preparation.**

ShadowVault is my curated knowledge repository built in Obsidian and synced with GitHub. It serves as a centralized system for capturing ideas, documenting learning, tracking projects, and connecting concepts across software engineering, machine learning, and technical interview preparation.

## 🧠 Vault Philosophy

ShadowVault is designed around the concept of a **connected knowledge system**, not a collection of isolated notes.

### Core Principles

* **Bidirectional Linking**

  * Connect related concepts across software engineering, machine learning, and research domains.
  * Enable context-driven learning through interconnected notes.

* **Maps of Content (MOCs)**

  * Create high-level navigation hubs for major topics.
  * Organize complex subjects into discoverable knowledge pathways.

* **Project-Centered Learning**

  * Link theory directly to implementation.
  * Bridge academic concepts with practical engineering solutions.

* **Daily Knowledge Capture**

  * Record insights, experiments, progress logs, and research findings.
  * Maintain a continuous learning timeline.
